# Design DNA — Clearway

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: editorial
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary: #2C5F4A → oklch(0.38 0.08 155) — deep sage green, trustworthy & calm
- Accent: #E8954A → oklch(0.70 0.14 55) — warm amber/copper, optimistic & human
- Surface: #F7F3EE → oklch(0.97 0.012 75) — warm cream/linen, comfortable & readable
- Text (body): oklch(0.28 0.02 80) — rich warm dark brown, not stark black
- Text (secondary): oklch(0.50 0.025 80) — mid warm brown for secondary content

### Font Pairing
- Display: Fraunces — weights 300–700 (warm, literary, optically-correct serif; perfect for editorial mood)
- Body: Lora — weights 400–600 (highly readable serif, great for long-form reading at larger sizes)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Full-width linen hero with an oversized italic pull-quote** — a large centered philosophical opener in Fraunces Italic before the structured content begins, like a New Yorker article opener. No gradient blobs — soft warm radial glow from above.
2. **Warm sage rule lines as dividers** — thin 1px sage green horizontal rules to divide sections, evoking a hand-crafted magazine rather than a web template. No card shadows or chunky SaaS borders.
3. **Amber/copper accent ink on key terms** — inline color on numbers and named topics (Social Security, Medicare) draws the eye like a printed highlighter, not a badge or button.
4. **Asymmetric editorial columns** — featured content uses a 60/40 split, not an equal-column grid. Matches editorial magazine layout, not a SaaS feature list.
5. **Paper-texture hero background** — layered OKLCH radial gradients at very low chroma, with SVG noise grain overlay, evoking warm indoor lighting rather than a sterile digital background.
